Common Questions

Everything you need
to know about BIH.

What exactly does Business Intelligence Holdings do? +
BIH is Jamaica's leading AI and data transformation firm. We work with Caribbean financial institutions and enterprises to build AI systems, data warehouses, and governance frameworks that turn fragmented data into real-time, trusted intelligence. Our work typically reduces reporting cycles by 70%, achieves full DPA (2020) compliance, and gives leadership real-time visibility into their operations — through four core service lines: Technology as a Service, AI Agent Automation, Data Warehouse & Intelligence, and Data Protection & Governance.
What is FHILE and which institutions is it built for? +
FHILE (First Historic Intelligence Lending Engine) is BIH's flagship AI-powered loan underwriting platform, purpose-built for Caribbean financial institutions — commercial banks, credit unions, microfinance lenders, and building societies. It has two core engines: an AI Underwriter that scores loan applications in under 4 minutes, and an Opportunity Scanner that identifies qualified borrowers already inside your existing portfolio. Implementation runs over a 6–8 month contract. Try the live simulation →
What is the CAIOR and how does it work? +
The Caribbean AI Opportunity & Readiness Score (CAIOR) is BIH's free, 10-question assessment that scores your organization across five dimensions: Data Infrastructure, Process Maturity, Technology Stack, Leadership Readiness, and Talent & Capability. Each dimension is scored out of 20 (100 total). You receive a band rating from Baseline through Platinum, a dimension breakdown, your top three gaps, and BIH's recommended transformation roadmap. It takes under 5 minutes. Take the assessment →
What does Jamaica's Data Protection Act (2020) require of my organization? +
Jamaica's DPA (2020) requires organizations that collect, store, or process personal data to: register with the Office of the Information Commissioner (OIC), appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO), conduct Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs), implement data classification and retention policies, establish breach response procedures, and obtain lawful consent for data processing. Non-compliance carries significant legal and reputational risk. BIH delivers full DPA compliance frameworks, outsourced DPO services, OIC registration support, and staff training through BII.
How long does a typical BIH engagement take? +
Engagement timelines depend on scope and complexity. As a guide: FHILE implementation contracts run 6–8 months. Data warehouse implementations typically run 3–6 months. DPA compliance frameworks are delivered in 6–12 weeks. AI Agent Automation pilots can go live in 4–8 weeks. All engagements begin with a free 30-minute Discovery Call where we assess your situation and give you an honest timeline estimate before any commitment is made.
Does BIH work with organizations outside Jamaica? +
Yes. While BIH is headquartered in Kingston, Jamaica (9th Floor, PanJam Building, 60 Knutsford Blvd), we serve clients across the Caribbean region. Our distributed engineering team — spanning Jamaica, the United States, and South Asia — allows us to support engagements across multiple time zones. We have delivered projects for institutions in Trinidad, Barbados, and across the Eastern Caribbean. If you're based outside Jamaica, book a Discovery Call and we'll assess fit.
What makes BIH different from a typical IT vendor or consulting firm? +
Three things. First, BIH is outcome-driven — we measure success in business results (faster reporting, cleaner compliance, better lending decisions), not feature delivery or billable hours. Second, BIH is governance-first — every system we build is auditable, documented, and designed to comply with Jamaica's DPA (2020) from day one. Third, BIH is built for this market — our team has 60+ combined years in Caribbean financial services. We understand JA credit bureaus, JCCUL structures, OIC requirements, and the specific data challenges facing regional institutions. We're not applying an overseas template to your market.
How do I get started with BIH? +
The first step is always a free 30-minute Discovery Call. On that call, we'll explore your current data situation, your leadership goals, and your biggest operational challenges. We'll tell you honestly what we think the right approach is — and whether BIH is the right fit. There's no obligation and no sales pressure.
